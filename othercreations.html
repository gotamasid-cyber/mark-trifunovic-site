Beyond Film

I Like to Make Things

Aside from making movies, I like to make other things, too. I like working with my hands, learning new skills, and using tools. I appreciate attention to detail, aesthetics, function and design. I like using discarded materials destined for disposal, and repurposing them for something new. This is a place for showcasing some of the things I have made that aren't films. (It hasn't stopped me from making films about them being made, though. Go figure.)
